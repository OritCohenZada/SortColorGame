You have to sort the bricks in the columns by color,
Each time you can move only one stone that is at the top of one of the columns to another column where there is a stone of the same color at the top and the column is still not full, or to an empty column.
During the game you can press the "I" key on the keyboard to open the instructions.
Additionally, you can press CTRL+Z to cancel the last action you did and press the "New Game" button to restart the game
pay attention: By performing these actions you lose a point...
